# 🧑‍💼 HR Interview Round

The HR round checks your **communication, attitude, and fit**. These answers are templates —
personalize them with your own story.

## 📄 Full Guide
- [HR Questions & Sample Answers](./01-HR-Questions.md)

## 🎯 Golden Rules
- Be **honest**, **positive**, and **specific**.
- Use the **STAR** method for behavioral questions (Situation, Task, Action, Result).
- Keep answers **1–2 minutes** — clear, not rambling.
- Always **research the company** before the interview.
